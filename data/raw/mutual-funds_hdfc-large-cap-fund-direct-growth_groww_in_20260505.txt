About HDFC Large Cap Fund Direct Growth
HDFC Large Cap Fund Direct Growth is a Equity Mutual Fund Scheme launched by HDFC Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 09 Dec 1999. Rahul Baijal is the Current Fund Manager of HDFC Large Cap Fund Direct Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹8,54,364 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 04 May 2026 is ₹1,192.37. The HDFC Large Cap Fund Direct Growth is rated Very High risk. Minimum SIP Investment is set to ₹100. Minimum Lumpsum Investment is ₹100. Exit load of 1% if redeemed within 1 year
