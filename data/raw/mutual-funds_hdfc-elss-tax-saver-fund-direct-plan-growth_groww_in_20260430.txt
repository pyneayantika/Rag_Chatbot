About HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund Direct Plan Growth
HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund Direct Plan Growth is a Equity Mutual Fund Scheme launched by HDFC Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 09 Dec 1999. Amar Kalkundrikar is the Current Fund Manager of HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund Direct Plan Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹8,54,364 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 29 Apr 2026 is ₹1,457.79. The HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund Direct Plan Growth is rated Very High risk. Minimum SIP Investment is set to ₹500. Minimum Lumpsum Investment is ₹500.
